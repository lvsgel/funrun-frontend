FAQ



What do I need to bring to the race kit collection?

Just bring your phone! Just show us the e-tickets on your JomRun app and youre good to go!

Can I collect the race kit on behalf of others?

Yes, you may! Just ask them to send a screenshot of their e-tickets to you and youll be able to collect for all your friends and family members.

I was unable to attend the event. Can I claim the medal?

The finisher medal will only be given to finishers. Only participants who have finished the race are entitled to the medal. Hope to see you at our next event!
